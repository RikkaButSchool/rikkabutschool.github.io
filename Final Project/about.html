About Me

It was fascinating to me how they would work and how they were developed.

I enjoyed learning about the different parts that built a computer and how they functioned. I learned how to do html and css to create websites and design them. I enjoy watching anime as it was something I would watch to pass the time like naruto or bleach. I spend time with friends whether it be gaming or going somewhere like the movies.
